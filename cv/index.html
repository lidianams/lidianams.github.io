Lidiana de Moraes

Ph.D. Student, Teaching Assistant, and Researcher

CV (Updated December 2018)

Education

  • PhD in in Literary, Cultural, and Linguistic Studies. University of Miami, Anticipated 2021.
  • MA in Literary Theory. Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), Distinction 2012.
  • Teaching Degree in Portuguese and English. Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), 2010.
  • BA in Social Communication: Journalism. Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul (PUCRS), 2009.

Teaching and Research Interests

  • Subaltern Studies (with an emphasis on gender representation).
  • Luso-Afro-Brazilian Literatures.
  • Postcolonialism and decolonization in the Lusophone world.
  • Transnational and Transatlantic relations in the cultural production of Lusophone countries (especially the African heritage in music, literature, soap operas, and cinema).
  • Comparative Literature.
  • Second Language Acquisition, Sociolinguistics, and Ideologies of Language.
