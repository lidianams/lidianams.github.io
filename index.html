Lidiana de Moraes

Phd Student, Teaching Assistant and Researcher at the University of Miami

My name is Lidiana de Moraes and I am from Brazil. Currently, I live in the United States where I am a third year Ph.D. student in Literary, Cultural, and Linguistic Studies, at the University of Miami. I hold a Master’s degree in Literary Theory (Pontifícia Universidade Católica do Rio Grande do Sul – PUCRS), and two undergraduate degrees, a Teaching Degree (in Portuguese and English Language and Literature – PUCRS) and a Bachelor’s in Social Communication (focused on Journalism – PUCRS).
